# Privacy Policy for Padma

**Last Updated: December 30, 2024**

## Introduction

Nemi Inc ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Padma application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data Only
Padma is designed with privacy in mind. **All data processing occurs locally on your device.** We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The App accesses the following information solely for its functionality:

- **System Information**: Hardware specifications, operating system details, CPU, RAM, and storage information for diagnostic purposes
- **Installed Applications**: List of startup programs for optimization features
- **File System Data**: Temporary files, cache files, and system junk for cleanup operations
- **Registry Information**: Windows registry entries for registry cleaning features (Windows only)
- **Network Configuration**: Local network settings for network optimization features

### No Data Transmission
- We do **NOT** collect personal identification information
- We do **NOT** transmit any data to external servers
- We do **NOT** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **NOT** share any information with third parties

## How We Use Information

All information accessed by Padma is used exclusively for:

1. **System Diagnostics**: Displaying your system specifications and health status
2. **Cleanup Operations**: Identifying and removing temporary/junk files
3. **Performance Optimization**: Managing startup programs and system resources
4. **Registry Maintenance**: Detecting and fixing registry issues
5. **Network Optimization**: Improving network performance settings

## Data Storage

- All scan results and settings are stored locally on your device
- No data is uploaded to cloud services or remote servers
- Uninstalling the App removes all associated data

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:
- Your information never leaves your computer
- No risk of data breaches from our servers
- You maintain complete control over your data

## Permissions Required

The App requires certain system permissions to function:

- **File System Access**: To scan and clean temporary files
- **Registry Access**: To scan and repair registry issues (Windows)
- **System Information Access**: To display hardware and software details
- **Administrator Privileges**: Some optimization features may require elevated permissions

## Children's Privacy

Padma does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.


## Consent

By using Padma, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
